Frequently ask questions

Q.

What is CAP?

Certified Accreditor Program (CAP) is an initiative designed to enable the Lagos State Government engage private sector professional as stakeholders in regulating the built environment. These partners will be known as Certified Accreditors (CAs). CAs will work with the Ministry and its Agencies, LASPPPA & LASBCA.

Q.

How can I become a Certified Accreditor (CA)?

A Certified Accreditor (CA) is a group of professionals that come together to form a Consortium. All CAs must have a minimum of 4 Registered Professionals depending on the grade

  • A Registered Architect (compulsory)
  • A Registered Civil/Structural Engineer (compulsory)
  • A Registered Mechanical Engineer (compulsory)
  • A Registered Electrical Engineer (compulsory)
    • Registered Builder
  • A Registered Town Planner
  • A Registered Geoscientist
All Parties to a CA must obtain a letter of recommendation from their respective professional body to be qualify.
Q.

Can an already existing registered Company/Firm register as a CA?

Yes, the Firm only need to provide proof having the required professionals as members of the Firm

Q.

Who are the professionals qualified for CAP?

  • i. A Registered Architect
  • ii. A Registered Civil/Structural Engineer
  • iii. A Registered Mechanical Engineer
  • iv. A Registered Electrical Engineer
  • v. A Registered Builder
  • vi. A Registered Town Planner
  • vii. A Registered Geoscientist
  • viii. Other Relevant Professional in the Built Environment

    • Q.

    Can I register as an individual?

    No, Professionals are to come together to register as a group.

    Q.

    Do CAs need to register with Corporate Affairs Commission as a Company?

    No, the professionals only need to come together and register for the CAP.

    Q.

    What is the registration fee for CAP?

    Yearly registration fee ranging from N50,000 and N200,000 depending on the Grade.

    Q.

    How many years' experience is required to become a CA?

    Minimum of 3 years' post Professional registration experience depending on the grade.

    Q.

    Why CAP?

    Lagos State is introducing the Certified Accreditor Programme as part of the reforms to restore confidence in the building construction industry by ensuring that all building projects within the state are adequately monitored.
